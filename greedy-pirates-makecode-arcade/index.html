Greedy Pirates - MakeCode Arcade

Greedy Pirates was a hackathon game my son and I worked on for a small MakeCode Arcade sponsored event. The theme was inspired by a summer camp game he played where kids would "battle" over islands. Besides the name however, most of the game was inspired by older side-scrolling beat-em ups on the Nintendo Entertainment System.
